# **App Name**: TimeTrack Pro

## Core Features:

- Attendance Logging: Allow admin to record daily in and out times for staff by selecting the staff name, month, and date.
- Advance Payment Recording: Allow admin to record advance payments for staff by selecting the staff name and entering the amount.
- Staff List Management: Maintain a comprehensive staff list.
- Salary Calculation: Show staff total working hours, salary amount, total advance, and balance.
- Admin Panel: Allow admin to add and remove staff, fix salary of staff per hour, and create passwords for staff.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep blue (#3F51B5) for a professional and reliable feel.
- Background color: Light blue-gray (#E8EAF6), providing a clean and calming backdrop.
- Accent color: Orange (#FF9800) to highlight important actions and calls to action.
- Font: 'Inter' sans-serif, for clear and modern readability in both headlines and body text.
- Use clean and simple icons from a standard library to represent common actions such as 'check-in', 'check-out', and 'payment'.
- Maintain a consistent and straightforward layout across all pages to ensure ease of navigation.
- Incorporate subtle transitions for loading and form submissions to enhance user experience.
